Lawrenceville Family Dentistry

Dr. Nayeem Bashar, welcome you and your family to experience caring and comfortable dental care. Since May 2007 our experienced staff has provided cosmetic, restorative and preventive dental care to Wilmington, NC and surrounding areas. Our dentist office is located in Lawrenceville at 3664 Club Drive in a free standing building which was designed with our patients in mind. Your smile is very important and we would like to help you keep that beautiful smile and maintain your dental health. We provide tooth whitening, implants, periodontal treatment, fillings, and dentures. Dr. Bashar is a MetLife provider of service and we also accept CareCredit along with Visa, MasterCard, and Discover payments.
